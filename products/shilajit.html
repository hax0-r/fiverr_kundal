Shilajit (purified) is an Ayurvedic medicine used in the indigenous system of Indian medicine. Shilajit is a medicine that has been used for centuries in Ayurvedic medicine. it is a dark brown powder or seepage from the rocks of the high mountains of the Himalayan between India and Nepal. it is also found in Afghanistan, Tibet, Russia, and northern Chile. in northern India it is known as Shilajatu, Salajit, Mummion or Mimi.

Shilajit is an important medicine listed in Ayurvedic Materia Medica, and it is used by Ayurvedic practitioners for the management of a variety of diseases. Shilajit is known as a magnificent medicine that holds the divine powers of the mountains. It is made up of rock minerals, rock humus and organic matter, which are compressed by layers of rock.
